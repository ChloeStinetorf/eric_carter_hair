Eric Carter grew up in a small Indiana town with a great imagination and a passion for cutting hair. At the age of seventeen he enrolled in Don Roberts Beauty School and finished top of his class. Eric began working at a upscale chic hair salon that molded his talent for up-styles and precision cutting. He began styling hair for theater shows, beauty models, and local events.

In 2003 Eric headed west and instantly began styling for runway as a platform artist. Eric learned the latest color techniques from top stylists in LA and Arizona, becoming an expert on the perfect shades of blonde. In 2006 Eric moved to New York City to expand his creativity and all that he had learned.

Eric's passion for hair is evident in all his creations whether it is beautiful color, a fashionable haircut, or flawless makeup. He has an exceptional talent for creating up-do styles that match the occasion perfectly. Eric has worked on photo shoots, theater productions, runway, TV, weddings, events, red carpet, print, and people who are just looking for a fantastic haircut.

Eric is a hair designer with a creative drive in the fashion inspired world and he loves what he does. He believes that continuing his education is essential and is always updating his craft to stay on top of the industry. Eric wants his clients to feel amazing! With some Midwest charm, out west exposure, and great talent, Eric can give you the hair you desire.
